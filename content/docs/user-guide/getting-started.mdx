## Prerequisites

-   Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet)
-   USDC stablecoins (minimum 10 USDC)

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Launch the NectaFi app
2. Click "Connect Wallet" and select your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet)
3. Approve the connection

## Step 2: Enable Your Smart Account

1. Click Enable to deploy your safe smart account
2. Sign once to activate automated yield optimzation

## Step 3: Deposit Funds

1. Enter the amount of USDC (min. 10 USDC).
2. Confirm the deposit in your wallet.

## Step 5: Activate Agents

1. Review and click "Activate"
2. Approve the activation transaction

## Step 6: Track Profits & Withdraw Anytime

-   Track earnings in real-time on your dashboard.
-   Withdraw anytime with no fees.
